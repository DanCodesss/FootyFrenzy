German media ripped into Man United loanee Jadon Sancho for his "poor" performance in Borussia Dortmund's loss to Hoffenheim.

Jadon Sancho, a Manchester United loanee, was called "the worst" by the German media following his lackluster performance in Borussia Dortmund's loss to Hoffenheim.

Sancho returned to Dortmund on loan from United in January but failed to leave a significant impact in Germany.

Initially, he started brightly with a couple of assists but has since struggled to contribute goals or assists.

German media criticized his recent performance, labeling him as one of the worst players in Dortmund's defeat to Hoffenheim.

The 23-year-old's return to Germany followed a fallout with United manager Erik ten Hag, leading to his departure.

United may consider selling Sancho in the summer, but Dortmund is unlikely to afford his high transfer fee.

To secure a spot in the England squad for Euro 2024, Sancho needs to improve his consistency significantly.
