Watch: Cristiano Ronaldo does an additional peculiar response to Lionel Messi's taunts.

Cristiano Ronaldo's opponents never stop making fun of him wherever he goes by bringing up Lionel Messi. Even though the Argentine decided to go to Major League Soccer rather than the Saudi Pro League, the Saudi supporters, as soon as they realized they might reach the Portuguese, have made sure that the Argentine remains very much a part of Cristiano Ronaldo's universe. On Sunday, Ronaldo's Al-Nassr defeated Al-Shabab 3-2; the 38-year-old scored the first goal from the penalty spot. Carlos Carvalho and Yannick Carrasco were surpassed by Anderson Talisca's double.

At the end of the game, Ronaldo was still receiving more ‘Messi’ taunts from the local crowd though, to which he cupped his ear and then seemed to make another gesture referencing his genitals, although quite what the exact translation is was unclear.
